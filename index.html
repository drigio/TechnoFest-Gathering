Formation of teams

  • All the Head teams like Sponsor Team, Finance Team etc will consist of 8 members. One member from each department will represent his/her department. The representative for the respective head team will be chosen by the President of student organisation and must be approved by respective department Teacher.

  • All the events will be organised and maintained by the respective department.

  • Each department will decide their own particular Event heads.

  • All the Publicity teams should be ready before hand. Their work will start immediately and must be completed atleast one month prior to the Event ie December.

  • The Head teams can have as many volunteers as they need.

  • We are moving our WhatsApp group to Telegram as there are many unwanted messages which can be avoided by using Telegram. All those who are interested and are volunteers/Event Heads/Head Team members must compulsory use Telegram and join the Telegram channel which will be created shortly.
